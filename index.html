What is RiseUI Project?

A project for riceDroid OS - where we aim to bring AOSP in a completely different way
in terms of UI and UX, the word "rise" was choosen to as the name of the project because it rhymes with the "rice"
and signifies this revision as our more serious approach in providing new user experience
(riceDroid was supposed to be a parody or just for fun operating system).
